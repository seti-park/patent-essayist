# Example

Everyone is watching the corporate structure. The merger. The board seats. The valuation multiples.

But patents describe what engineers are actually building. And these two patents (one making the car transparent to satellite signals, the other making satellite networks transparent to standard devices) were developed in parallel, published weeks apart, and fit together without a single redundant part.

The corporate story is separation. But the patents read like convergence.

If Musk succeeds in what these filings suggest (Tesla cars providing the data, xAI providing the intelligence, SpaceX providing the global connectivity), the result isn't three companies. It's one closed-loop ecosystem that no single-sector competitor can replicate.

Which story reflects what's actually being built? 🤔
