# Example

The signal worth watching is whichever teardown or disclosure first quantifies the leakage Tesla just engineered out, and whether the number is large enough to be a moat or small enough to be a footnote. Until then, the more durable fact is the one already on the record: Tesla looked at the steel tax everyone pays and decided it did not have to.
