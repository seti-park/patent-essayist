# Example

To see the trade, you have to see the bind the designer is in. At high speed the rotor wants to tear itself apart, and the force trying to do that climbs with the square of the speed, so a motor spinning twice as fast pulls four times as hard. Steel struts are the cheap, strong answer that keeps the magnets seated. The problem is that steel is magnetic.
